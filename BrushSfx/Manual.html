Brush Sound Effects

This plugin adds sound effects simulating a pencil or a pen when you draw

Configuration

  • On krita's menu bar go to `Settings > Dockers > Brush SFX`.
  • The SFX option enables and disables the sound effects. On by default
  • The Sound Choice option lets you choose which sound you want for your brush.

Sound Options

Currently there are 2 sound options:

  • pencil-1(default)
  • pen-1
