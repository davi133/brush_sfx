Brush Sound Effects

This plugin adds sound effects simulating a pencil or other art tools when you draw. You can assign different sounds for different brush presets. Krita's default brushes already comes with sounds assigned to.

Configuration

  • On krita's menu bar go to `Tools > Scripts > Brush SFX` or press `F8`to open the plugin's configuration.
  • The Sound Effects option enables and disables the sound effects. On by default
  • The Brush sound option lets you choose which sound you want for your brush.
  • Sound for eraser sets if a sound will be played on the eraser mode
  • The Eraser sound option lets you choose which sound you want for your eraser.
  • The Use different sound on current preset option lets you use different settings for the current brush preset

Sound Options

These are the available sound effects:

  • [no sound] (doesn't count)
  • eraser
  • pencil
  • pen
  • paintbrush
  • airbrush
  • spray can
